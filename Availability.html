This lovely four bedroom, three and a half bath, waterfront home with panoramic view offers a private beach with a private fishing pier, swing, deck, arcade basketball, and ping pong table. The entire house has been recently renovated with contemporary style design. The gourmet kitchen allows you to host amazing meals. A large dining table and island counter allows for plenty of room to enjoy your gourmet meals. Enjoy relaxing on the large outdoor deck with plenty of seating overlooking the lake to enjoy the most amazing sunsets. The home is fully equipped with bedding, towels, and amenities.

The property is located on a small cul-de-sac right off highway 20. This large 2400 square foot house is equipped with central heating and cooling with plenty of natural light with large windows to enjoy the lake view. There are plenty of parking for your cars and your water toys.

This is a spectacular wildlife setting. You can view a variety of geese, ducks, cranes, and white pelicans. Clear Lake is the largest natural lake in California. Its green hue originates from all the algae that grows in the lake. This area is a fisherman's delight, voted one of the best bass fishing spots in the world. You will find great fishing for bass, catfish, and blue gill right off the private piers. Large bass and catfish are commonly caught off the pier during peak fishing season (March-July). This contemporary style home is ready to serve you and your family with the comfort and convenience of your home.
